Who Am I

About Me

I'm an Entrepreneurship graduate from the University of Caloocan City and a certified General Virtual Assistant, based in the Philippines. My background isn't purely administrative — it started in business operations and finance, which is where I learned to treat every task, no matter how small, as something that affects a real outcome.

As Finance Head for a student-run business implementation project, I managed ₱109,000 in operating capital — tracking every peso of revenue and expense, recovering the full capital, and closing the project with over ₱40,000 in net profit. That experience, working alongside product, marketing, and sales teams, is what shaped how I approach VA work now: organized, accountable, and focused on the result.

Through my General Virtual Assistant training, I built on that foundation with hands-on practice in lead generation, data entry and scraping, calendar and inbox management, and social content scheduling. I'm looking to bring that same analytical, detail-first approach to a remote team that needs a dependable extra pair of hands.
